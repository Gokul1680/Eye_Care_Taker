*Please read the below instructions properly to avoid eye strains and Back Pain due to attending online class:

  1. Please Attend your classes through laptop and avoid Mobile phones since it is more dangerous to your eye and health too.
  2. While attending online class through laptops, follow proper procedures which are described below.
  3. Make sure that the top of the laptop screen is at eye level to avoid tilting your head forward or back, as this will stress your neck and shoulder muscles when sitting at it.
  4. Ensure that the screen is placed at arm's length from your eyes when you are sitting.
  5. Position your keyboard at the level of your elbows so that your hands and wrists are straight.
  6. Keep your head elevated and Take deep breaths.
  7. Exercise your hands, eyes and neck
  8. Follow 20 20 20 Rule:

    • For every 20 minutes, take a 20 second break by looking at an object which is 20 feet away

This site will help you to follow 20/20/20 Rule by reminding you to take a break after every 20 minutes. So open this site in a tab and Click Start button and then go to any other tab to resume your online class.

Note : This Site will work only in laptops/computers and refresh this page after every 4 hours.
